FaKe Extension

  1. 1Download and install Tampermonkey.
  2. 2Download the latest release of the FaKe extension from GitHub.
  3. 3Extract the zip and open script.js with Notepad.
  4. 4Click the + button and open a new userscript.
  5. 5Copy and paste script.js, replacing the old contents.
  6. 6Click file and then save. Done!

Extension Usage

  1. 1Navigate to a Tagalog news article.
  2. 2Pin Tampermonkey to your browser's address bar.
  3. 3Once pinned, click the Tampermonkey icon.
  4. 4Under Fake News Detector, click Detect Fake News.
  5. 5Wait for the system to process the article you're reading.
  6. 6Profit!
